# Privacy Policy — YouTube: Don't Recommend Channel

_Last updated: 2026-07-13_

This extension is designed to collect **no personal data**.

## What it stores

- A list of the channels you choose to block, and your settings (native-feedback
  toggle, keyboard shortcut). This is saved **locally in your browser** using
  `chrome.storage.local`. It never leaves your device except via features you
  explicitly use (the Export button, which saves a file where you choose, and
  Chrome's own optional sync if you have extension sync enabled).

## What it does not do

- It does **not** collect, transmit, or sell any personal or browsing data.
- It contains **no analytics, tracking, or advertising**.
- It sends **nothing** to the developer or any third-party server.

## Network activity

The only outbound activity is the optional **"Also tell YouTube"** feature. When
enabled, the extension clicks YouTube's own built-in "Don't recommend channel"
menu item on your behalf — exactly as if you clicked it yourself. That request
goes to YouTube (Google) under your own YouTube session; the developer neither
sees nor receives any of it. You can disable this feature in the extension popup.

## Permissions

- **storage** — to save your blocklist and settings locally.
- **host access to `youtube.com`** — to add the block button, hide videos from
  blocked channels, and (if enabled) trigger YouTube's native feedback.
